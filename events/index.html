Back To School Supply Drive

Since 2014 the MAYA Foundation, Inc. in partnership with From One Hand to Another, the Reggie/Pamela Williams Foundation and Youth, Excel and Advancement, LLC. have been able to to provide book bags full of school supplies for over 700 youth in Richmond, Petersburg and other surrounding counties. These book bags were given so that the students would have the means to have a productive and successful school year.

Collection for the 2017-2018 will begin July 2017.

Annual Turkey and Food Drive

The Annual Turkey and Food Drive began back in 2012 with the start of the MAYA Foundation. Since then, with the help of family, community members, local grocery stores and corportate sponsors; we have been able to provide over 400 families with food baskets during the holidays.

Collection of food and monetary donations will begin in October 2017.

If you are interested in donation to either drives, please feel free to contact our program coordniator at brittany@maya.foundation or you can drop all donations off at 910 S. Harrison Street Richmond, VA 23220.
